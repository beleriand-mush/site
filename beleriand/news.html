On Beleriand MUSH, all game policy and world information is stored in the NEWS files. Thus, in MUSH lingo, NEWS means Game Information. On Beleriand, NEWS covers a vast array of topics. It has been the work over many years of many Game Administrators past and present. It is here available in hypertext format for your ease of perusal.


            Administration      administrator ranks, chain of command, ZMOs
Building      descriptions, exits, rooms, tips
Coding      tips for coding (puppets)
Combat      our combat system, player stats
Species      the various kinds of beings in Beleriand


The NEWS files are currently in the process of a major overhaul, rewrite, and general preparation. Please be patient as we add more NEWS.
