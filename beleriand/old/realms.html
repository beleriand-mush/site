Realms of Beleriand

For the most up-to-date listing of Culture Wardens, please connect to the MUSH and use the ``-adm/cw'' command.

Elf Realms

Mithrim
In Hithlum in the North, Mithrim is the seat of the High King of the Noldor, Fingolfin, and Fingon his son, and their people are hardy and valiant. Mithrim is inhabited by both Noldor and Sindar.
Acting Warden:  Moradan
Gondolin
Gondolin is the Hidden Kingdom of the Elves, encircled by mountains. Ruled by Turgon, son of Fingolfin, and protected by the Eagles, it is a safe refuge for both Noldor and Sindar.
Warden:  Tavarilyn
Nargothrond
In West Beleriand, Nargothrond is the greatest of the Noldorin realms, ruled by Finrod Felagund. A well-guarded realm, it includes the island tower of Tol Sirion and the great Caverns of Narog.
Warden:  Finrod
Feanorian
In East Beleriand, the Feanorian realm is a union of the realms the seven proud Noldorin sons of Feanor, Maedhros and his brothers. (This includes Himring, Maglor's Gap, Himlad, Thargelion, and Estolad.)
Warden:  Maedhros
Doriath
The great Sindarin kingdom of Beleriand, Doriath is ruled by Thingol and wise Melian. A guarded realm of the Sindarin Elves.
Warden:  Beleg
Falas
The Falas is the coastal realm of the Sindar ruled by Cirdan the Shipwright.
Warden:  Narbelithil
Ossiriand
Heavily wooded, Ossiriand is the home of the Laiquendi, the peaceful, rustic Green-Elves.
Warden:  Aranar

Dwarf Realms

Belegost
On the northern side of Mount Dolmed in the Blue Mountains lies Belegost, or Gabilgathol, home of Azaghal and the Broadbeam Dwarves.
Warden:  None
Nogrod
On the southern side of Mount Dolmed in the Blue Mountains lies Nogrod, or Tumunzahar, home of Naugladur and the Firebeard Dwarves.
Warden:  Tulir

Human Realms

Dorthonion
The Highlands of Dorthonion are home to Bregolas and the nomadic First House of the Edain.
Warden:  Bregolas
Brethil
The Forest of Brethil is the home of Halmir and the Haladin (the Second House of the Edain), and also to the Druedain.
Warden:  None
Dor-lomin
Dor-lomin in Hithlum is home to Hador and the great Third House of the Edain.
Warden:  Hador

Other Realms

Angband
The Hells of Iron, home of evil, stronghold of the north where dwells Morgoth the Evil Vala and his minions.
Warden:  Thuringwethil
Crissaegrim
High mountain peaks from whence the Eagles of Manwe with Thorondor their lord help the oppressed people of Beleriand.
Valinor
Land of the West across the Great Sea, where dwell the great Ainur and the Calaquendi.
