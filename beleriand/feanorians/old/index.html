Welcome to the home of the Sons of Fëanor and their followers-- known as the Fëanorians-- the first line of defense on the Eastern Marches against the hordes of Morgoth, god of evil. Join us as we fight Orcs and Balrogs, Werewolves and Dragons, in the ongoing struggle to recover what was stolen: the greatest treasures of Elvenkind, the Holy Jewels of Light, the Silmarilli!


These scrolls contain a lot of great information about the Fëanorian realm. Fëanorians are encouraged to read everything to get a good feel for the culture, and any other citizen of Beleriand who wants to find out more about us is certainly welcome. Indeed, while these scrolls were mainly intended for players of Beleriand MUSH, they also serve as a general fan site for the Sons of Fëanor from J.R.R. Tolkien's The History of Middle-earth series.
