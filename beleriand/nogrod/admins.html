Admins

Here you may find some brief info on the culture administrators of Nogrod. This is a team of players in Nogrod who build, organize TinyPlots, and in other ways keep the place running.

NAUGLADUR - Warden
Naugladur serves as Culture Warden of Nogrod. As such, he is the head honcho of Nogrod and all things Dwarven. He is in charge of recruiting and also very actively creates and participates in TinyPlots, and sometimes dabbles in code. Naugladur is Lord of Nogrod. He has formerly appeared as Agarhaz and Kuzadeh.		 DRÓR - Support
Drór has created -no info, this website, much of Nogrod's local code and building, and has been involved in Nogrod in varying capacities, including many times Warden and Vice-Warden, since early 1996. He started in 1995 as builder of Belegost and has since served the MUSH in a variety of roles including Royal (Ëonwë, 1996-1997; 2001-2002) and Wizard (Ulmo, 2002-present). As Lord Narag, he is lord of the Narag house and faction. He has also role-played as Maedhros, Cúrufin, and Sauron.
VOGAN - Support
Vogan is a former Warden of Nogrod, and is still around to help provide leadership and continuity. He has been involved in Nogrod since 1999 and has been awarded Feature status. He has previously appeared as Barzyn, Telchar, and Tamalin.
