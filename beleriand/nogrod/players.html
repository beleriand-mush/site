Players

These are the current members of Nogrod who have been set up, and information about them, including the descriptions and histories they have written themselves. Last updated: 9/28/07. Please let Drór know of any changes.

DRÓR (ÛMAHAL)
Faction: Narag
Rank: Noble, 1st Class (House Narag)
Liege: King Naugladur Zirak
Profession: Wine Merchant (and Lord Chancellor)
Guild Rank: Master
Description: Drór is an old Dwarf, short and shifty but with an air of some assumed nobility and, certainly, some pride. A grey beard forks from his chin onto his chest, but no lower, for this is no Longbeard, but a Firebeard of Nogrod. He has sharp black eyes and bushy grey eyebrows set in his weatherbeaten face. His hands, too, are calloused and worn. Drór wears a black tunic, decorated with a great silver seal: the coat of arms of the King of the Hollowbold, which this vassal displays proudly. But over all of this he wears an old grey cloak which covers his arms and back but parts in the front like an overcoat, and the grey hood attached is thrown over his head. You can see baggy woolen black pants upon his legs, and black boots upon his feet.
History: Unknown.

KHALA
Faction: Baraz
Rank: Serf, 1st Class
Liege: Telchar, Lord Baraz
Profession: Carpenter
Guild Rank: Apprentice
Description: The figure before you stands no more than a few inches over four feet tall, yet somehow appears a bit physically imposing. Khala's long straight brown hair, which hangs loose and free, has a touch of red in it, accentuating her tanned, well-muscled features. Her beard is soft and intricately woven with small colorful beads. Her complexion is rosey, her eyes crystal blue and sparkling and her mouth is perfectly shaped. She has a warm and bubbly personality. She wears practical garb, if a bit unremarkable..a reddish colored woolen tunic and leather leggings. On her feet are soft leather knee-high boots.
History: Unknown

VOGAN
Faction: Baraz
Rank: Serf, 1st Class
Liege: Telchar, Lord Baraz
Profession: Diplomat
Guild Rank: Master
Description: Your eyes fall onto a Khazad, with fiery bright red hair and platted beard encrusted with gems, which falls lightly to his chest. His eyes are bright blue, and are topped with eyebrows of a slightly darker hue of red. His hands hang lightly at his side, and seem strangely lost as they are kept still.

He is dressed in a dark tunic and breaches which are tucked into black boots. Upon his left breast is the red emblem of the Baraz faction, and about his kneck there hangs a small gold chain, and attached to his belt is a knife of high quality.
History: Unknown

GAMLIN
Faction: Zirak
Rank: Noble, 3rd Class (House Azan)
Liege: Kuzadeh, Lord Azan
Profession: Merchant
Guild Rank: Master
Description: Gamlin, to some, would appear to be your 'stereotypical' Khazad. Four feet and a few inches and stout with a slight plumping he stands stocky and sturdy. A long black beard, clumps of which have been neatly braided and tied off with a black cord, which reaches down to his belt and the black hair, also braided in places, that falls down over his shoulders and ends at his shoulder blades. His eyes are of a bluish grey and sit deeply in his skull beneath two black bushy eyebrows and flanking a plump nose. Adorned in a simple black tunic and dark brown breaches, that are held up by a large shiny buckled leather belt and is tucked into his big scuffed boots.
History: Unknown

NAUGLADUR (KHAZÂD-UZBAD)
Faction: Zirak
Rank: Noble, King Class (House Zirak)
Liege: None
Profession: King
Guild Rank: None
Description: His beard and his stature mark this one as a dwarf. He stands slightly more than four feet tall his beard is nearly half that by itself. Careful and intricate braids worked with beads that appear to be made of diamonds, with a handful of rubies and onyxes as well, lie across his barrel chest and belly, reaching well below his waist. Roughly half of the hair in his beard is confined in the braids, the remainder spreads out between them like wispy flames. He has flame-red hair atop his head, though the beard is rather a darker shade. The skin of his face, at least what is visible of it above the beard, is rough and weathered. Green eyes with flecks of brown stare out of his ruddy countenance. His nose is rather hawkish and his brow seems to be beset by a permanent scowl. His build is stocky, almost squat but shows clear evidence of great strength. Utterly lacking the lithe grace of the elves he somehow still manages to seem balanced and poised. His is the grace of the earth and his manner owes more to boulder and mountain than tree or star.

His dress is that of a dwarf-lord of the highest rank. Fine leather boots on his feet, a kilt of white leather about his waist and on his torso, a most exquisite hauberk of double-linked mail. The mail coat is set with jewels in the alternating rows of its rings. Dozens of tiny diamonds shimmer in stripes across his shoulders and down the sleeves to his elbows. The chest and skirts of the knee length byrnie boast alternating stripes set with rubies and black onyxes. A longsword hangs in a jeweled scabbard at his side and a full helm, the visor worked in the likeness of some horrifying beast, glitters in gilded splendor where it hangs tied at his belt.
History: Unknown
